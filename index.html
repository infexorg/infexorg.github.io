Serving the community

INFEX was founded to provide tools, data, and services to the Star Citizen community. From comprehensive planetside maps to detailed ship statistics, we have them all, for the public to freely access.

Watch the recruitment video

Project Axi

Axi is a comprehensive map of points of interest on all planetary bodies. This project is INFEX's primary objective and we intend to maintain this project for as long as it is useful.

Project Jetstream

Jetstream is basically a space GPS. It is designed to integrate with Axi, providing quick and easy acces to any location marked in Axi.

Future projects

INFEX is always looking for new ways to support the community. Planned projects include a comprehensive database of ship statistics, such as acceleration and turning times.
